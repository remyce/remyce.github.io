Hello, I’m Remyce, a front-end web developer, designer and student based in London.

Having studied Interactive Art and Design, I’ve always had a love for the arts and technology. I first began coding through editing Tumblr themes. From then I knew I wanted to delve further into Web Development to build useful but life changing web applications. After a few online coding courses, I attended a three month coding bootcamp. This pushed me out of my comfort zone quicker than Sheldon realising someone was sitting in his sofa spot. Nevertheless it was the challenge I was searching for.

I am currently studying a BA in Creative Arts (part-time) and available for full time work and internships. Want to chat, collaborate? Contact me.
