Aura was inspired by the fascinating condition Synaesthesia which is best described as a 'union of the senses'.
Due to our creative backgrounds, Ali, Sharon and I tried to create an experience which focused on the joined senses of seeing sound as colours.

Our users are presented with a choice of sounds to play. Once they have done so, they can listen to the sound and pick a colour they feel represents it. After they have submitted their selection they are then taken to the results page. The results page displays a graphic animation which represents the sound's genre and the selected colours as a background animation.
