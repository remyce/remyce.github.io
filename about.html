Hello, I’m Remyce, a front-end web developer and designer based in London.

Having studied Interactive Art and Design, I’ve always had a love for the arts and technology. I first began coding through editing CMS (Content Management System) themes such as Tumblr. I wanted to delve further into Web Development in order to build useful but life changing web applications. So after a few online coding courses, I attended a three month coding bootcamp. This pushed me out of my comfort zone quicker than Sheldon realising someone was sitting in his sofa spot. Nevertheless it was the challenge I was searching for.

I would like to work with a collaborative team where I’ll be exposed to both creative direction and software development. I am currently studying a BA in Creative Arts part-time and available for full time work or internships.
